AI Job Risk Assessment

1. Does your job involve repetitive or routine tasks?

2. Do you use a computer for most of your work?

3. Are data analysis or reporting part of your job?

4. Does your company discuss automation or AI in meetings?

5. Have you noticed software replacing manual tasks in your workplace?

6. Is your job considered highly creative or innovative?

7. Do you often interact with customers or clients face-to-face?

8. Are you responsible for making complex decisions under uncertainty?

9. Do you perform physical work that cannot be automated easily?

10. Have you received training on new technology in the past year?
